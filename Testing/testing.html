Testing the App

Overview:

The Web App was developed for an Elitmus Assignment.
The Quiz requires that the player can request, answer and skip questions if allowed, certain questions can only be answered if they are at a location provided to the API to be checked.

To ensure that the app is reliable and performs well, we will use a variety of testing methods to assess the robustness of its features.
